History and Rules

History

Zloutch used to be a very popular game among pirates and sailors during the 16th c. They used to bet money (pieces of Heihgt), shots … or even kicks in the butt. For example, if you are 6 players: the winner gives 6 shots/kicks and receives none of them, the 2nd gives five and takes one, the 3rd one give 4 and receives two and so on until the loser who takes 6 shots/kicks. That was a good way to kill the time when lost on sea or when there were no Spanish galleon to assail...

Table of Rules

The winer is the first player to gather 10,000 Pieces of Eight (Spanish currency used in the 16th Century by pirates as their reference currency to trade in the Caribbean islands).
